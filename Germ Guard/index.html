Shop by Conditions

Athlete’s Foot Treatment

Athlete’s foot causes itching, redness, and flaking between the toes due to a fungal infection. Our podiatrist recommends antifungal treatments that target the root cause, reduce discomfort, and prevent recurrence.

Nail Fungus Treatment

Nail fungus leads to yellow, thickened, or brittle nails. Our treatment combines clinically proven antifungal products with expert care plans that target the infection beneath the nail surface.

Calluses & Corns Treatment

Calluses and corns develop due to pressure or friction on your feet. We offer gentle, effective removal options including podiatrist-approved tools and strategies.

Plantar Warts Treatment

Plantar warts appear on the soles of the feet and can cause pain while walking. Our care plans target the wart at the source and help in safe removal.

Shoe Odour Treatment

Unpleasant shoe odour often results from bacterial or fungal build-up. We offer antimicrobial sprays and hygiene routines that eliminate odour.

Diabetic Foot Treatment

Diabetic feet require extra care to prevent ulcers and infections. Our kits include cleansing and moisturizing solutions for sensitive skin.
